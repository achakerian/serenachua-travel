How it works

You brief me.
I plan the days.

No flights or hotels to book here — you've sorted those. My job is what happens in between: a day-by-day plan for the city you're in, built around how you actually like to travel.

01 · Brief

Tell me the essentials

City, hotel, the dates you're free, and a budget. Then the personal part — when you wake, what you're into, how you like to eat.

02 · Plan

I build your days

I map each day around your pace and interests, book anything that needs booking, and keep every choice inside your budget.

03 · Itinerary

You get your plan

A clean day-by-day itinerary — strict to the quarter-hour, or options you pick from as you go. Tweaks included.
